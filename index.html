Global Weather Map

This interactive map provides a comprehensive view of the world's weather patterns. You can explore different climatic features across various regions, providing insights into temperature, humidity, precipitation, and more.

Average Temperature

This chart displays the global average temperature. The data points can help identify trends over time, indicating whether the world is experiencing a period of warming or cooling.

Precipitation Levels

This bar chart represents precipitation levels in different regions around the world. Comparing these levels can help identify areas that are prone to droughts or floods.

Humidity Heatmap

This heat map shows humidity levels worldwide, giving a clear picture of areas with high or low humidity. This information is vital for understanding weather patterns and predicting potential climate changes.

Wind Speed Trends

This line chart illustrates the trends of wind speeds over time in different locations across the globe. Analyzing these trends is crucial for predicting weather patterns and potential storm formations.

Cloud Coverage and Visibility

This scatter plot displays the relationship between cloud coverage and visibility. The plot can reveal how cloudiness can affect visibility in various parts of the world.

UV Index Levels

This visualization represents the UV Index levels around the world. It's essential for understanding the potential risks of sun exposure in different regions.
