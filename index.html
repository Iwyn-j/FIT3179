Earthquake Visualization

Global Earthquake Map

This map provides a global view of earthquakes, indicating affected areas. By visualizing the data, we gain insights into patterns and can identify regions with higher seismic activity. The color intensity and points on the map correspond to the magnitude of the earthquakes, providing a quick overview of the most affected areas.
